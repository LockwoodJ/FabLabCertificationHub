Screwdrivers Training

Use screwdrivers to install and remove fasteners without damaging screws, parts, or your hands.

What You Need to Learn

  • Match screwdriver tip to fastener.
  • Keep pressure aligned with screw axis.
  • Use controlled torque.
  • Store drivers in the correct location.

Certification Focus

This page prepares you for the hands-on check. Passing the quiz does not replace the instructor sign-off.

Ask before acting: If the setup does not match the training, stop and ask your instructor.
Real FabLab Scenario: The screwdriver tip keeps slipping out of the screw. What should happen? Stop and choose the correct driver size/type.

Student Procedure

  1. Identify the screw type and size.
  2. Choose the matching screwdriver tip.
  3. Support or clamp the workpiece.
  4. Apply steady downward pressure while turning.
  5. Stop if the screw strips or the driver slips.
  6. Return the screwdriver to labeled storage.

Before You Start

  • Tip matches fastener.
  • Work supported.
  • Pressure aligned.
  • Tool returned.

Stop Immediately If...

  • The tool makes an unusual sound.
  • The material moves unexpectedly.
  • You smell burning, see smoke, or notice heat where it should not be.
  • You are unsure what the next step should be.
