Level 4 • Leadership

Help the FabLab run safely and smoothly.

Level 4 students model professional habits, help classmates prepare, keep the space organized, and know when to involve the instructor.

Leadership Looks Like...

  • Using calm, clear language when reminding peers about safety.
  • Helping classmates prepare files without doing the work for them.
  • Reporting damaged tools, missing supplies, or unsafe behavior.
  • Supporting cleanup, organization, and project documentation.
  • Knowing that only the instructor gives final approval for machine use.
