Purpose of This Agreement

The FabLab contains tools and equipment that require responsibility, attention, and respect. This contract is required before any certification is valid. By signing it, you agree to follow all safety procedures, use only tools you are certified and approved to use, and stop work when something seems unsafe.

Important: Completing this contract does not automatically certify you on any tool. You must still complete the required training, pass the certification quiz, and complete the hands-on instructor check.
